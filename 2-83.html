An Affair

x.hy (2007-12-18) 兴趣, 英语

I went to the post office this morning, I sent a parcel to xiaohou.

There were fewer people in the post office than usual.

At that moment the door was opened and a foreigner carried a wrap came in.

Then he said something in English, but the guard could not understand different language,

Just then one of us asked the foreigner:

‘May I help you?’

The foreigner repeated his trouble, the wrap was spoiled before he received it, He asked the man, where are take order with this, The man answered: ‘upstairs’.

Apart from ‘upstairs’, I could speak nothing.

I had better learn English hard, it is necessary.
